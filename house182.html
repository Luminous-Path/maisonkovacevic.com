Prefabricated House 182
182 m² 4 3
What's included

This House from Maison Kovacevic is 182 square meters in size. It is a superb three-bedroom family home with a large living room connected to an open floor kitchen and a large dining area, perfect for family gatherings. Upstairs there is plenty of space with three large bedrooms, one of which has its own bathroom and a closet. The factory package includes external insulation walls, internal walls, roof structure constructions, windows, and entrance doors.
